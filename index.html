Autosave clipboard data

Are you a Search addict? If Yes, then Search Bubble is for you. If No, then Search Bubble is still for you. Search Bubble automatically saves your clipboard data to its history with your authorization, in order to allow you to search from anywhere. If you are currently browsing the web and come across words your would love to search for, you simply need to copy the words to your clipboard and they are automatically stored by Search Bubble.

Search with a single click

Search Bubble allows you to search easily from anywhere at anytime. You can search by clicking the bubble at any time, with a single click on your clipboard history or by directly searching for what you want. That's how simple it is.
